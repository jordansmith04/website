About Me

DevOps, Performance, Development and Automation

My experience was built from the ground up. After completing a coding bootcamp, I started as an automation test engineer at Comcast. Having excelled in that position, I was moved into performance testing. During this time, I developed a user-interface and infrastructure performance testing framework (single-handedly, might I add), sparking my drive for development. Following that passion, I began my journey into development for a couple years at Charter communications. Striving to learn as much as I can, I was exposed to the cloud and DevOps. My passion evolved. I completed a degree in Cloud Computing. Today, I am fully entrenched in learning new technologies and streamlining DevOps practices. Making an impact on each company I have the pleasure to work for.

I believe my experience makes me a valuable member of any team. I know how to integrate and streamline the Software Development Lifecycle at all stages.
